This release brings vertical drag-and-drop sorting for sites, Node.js version support, PHP version enhancements, and a series of UI and workflow improvements.

- **Vertical drag-and-drop sorting** for sites list
- **Node.js version support** in site creation
- **PHP 7.4 and min version enforcement** for Laravel projects
- **UI/UX improvements** and refactoring
- **Performance and stability fixes**
